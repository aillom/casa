# Context Capsule: legacy-modernization

## Use When

Changing unclear, old, highly coupled or poorly tested modules.

## Load

- `.casa/capabilities/skills/legacy-archaeologist/SKILL.md`
- `.casa/capabilities/workflows/modernize-legacy-module.workflow.md`
- `.casa/kernel/standards/legacy.md`
- `.casa/modernization/discovery/playbook.md`
- `.casa/modernization/baselining/playbook.md`

## Watch

- Existing behavior to preserve.
- Hidden dependencies and runtime entry points.
- Missing characterization tests.
- Safe seams and rollback options.

## Validate

- Characterization or smoke tests.
- Legacy safety quality gate.
- Evidence ledger with preserved behavior notes.
